About Me

Technology speaks to me a lot because it is what we interact with on a daily basis and I like to stay informed on the latest and greatest. I keep up with tech blogs, browse through Kickstarter for the craziest (and even simple why-didn't-I-think-of-that) ideas, and interact with others like me. I also like to be hands-on as I like to carefully tear apart devices to see the inner-workings and building computers, both of which probably stems from my love for Legos.

Staying active is important to me because not only I want to stay healthy, I like to learn new sports. I'm not the type that actively keep up with professional sports (although I do cheer for my hometown San Francisco Giants, Golden State Warriors, and San Francisco 49ers!) but when given the opportunity to participate in a new sport, I jump at it. However, two of my favorite sports that I play is basketball and dragonboating. Basketball because I grew up playing it and dragonboating because there are almost no other sport in the world where you have at least 20 fellow teammates striving for one goal.

Between technology and sports, there is one commonality and that is I like to teach people when it comes to either subjects. I like informing friends what's the latest and coolest device or idea that's out. I like going into explaining how certain technology work. I like coaching and leading the dragonboat team, getting the best out of people.
